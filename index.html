BlockHub DAO

BlockHubDAO is a VC DAO of experienced people deeply involved in the Blockchain industry. We are close to 60 members, all of us with backgrounds in Finance, Marketing, IT and software engineering. Some of us are leading and working for major firms in the crypto ecosystem. After 5 years of carefully growing our community, we created BlockHubDAO in order to pool our network, knowledge, and resources to contribute more effectively to the industry and enable us to support promising upcoming projects. We're not just here to fund projects, but to participate with our community, we are passionate about crypto and trying to make the space a bit better everyday.
